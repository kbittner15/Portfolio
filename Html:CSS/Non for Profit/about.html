About Us

St. Clement Pope OLCA Track Team has been serving the southeastern Queens area for over 20 years. It has partnered with Our Lady's Catholic Academy and from neighboring communities. Participant practice and competition from September to June of the year. And hold some fun activities over the summer. They compete during CYO Cross Country Season in October and November, CYO Indoor Season from January to March, followed by CYO Outdoor Season from April to June.

Through the varied mediums of sports and other programs, we promote participation and sportsmanship with the leadership necessary to form a well-rounded individual, who will be prepared to live in a society with honor, dignity, and kindness.

Biannually, the Team also hosts an October Fund Raising Walk run, as well as numerous other small events over the year. Under the leadership of Coach Robert Pasqual, Coach Morgan and Mrs. Morgan, the St Clement Pop OLCA are a force to be reckoned with. They are small but mighty.
